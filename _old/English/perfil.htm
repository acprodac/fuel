:: Description ::

 
Established in 1987 through the association of the engineers Osmar dos Santos Fragata and Luiz Augusto de Arruda Miranda, coming from highly regarded installing and designing companies from Thermal Systems.

:: Objective ::

Since PRO DAC AR CONDICIONADO LTDA establishment it has been continuously acting with the objective to always offers to the customers the best available technological standard in the internal and external markets, providing differentiated services, searching perfect adequacy of its installations with the customers real necessities.

Following these principles that today are a work philosophy, it comes providing through computerization, a high level of satisfaction regarding the cost-benefit in its construction works, counting for this on technical resources and specialized staff available in its structure.
