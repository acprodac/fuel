:: Activities ::

PRO DAC AR CONDICIONADO LTDA provides specialized services of installation on derivatives from Thermal Engineering related to Central Air Conditioning Systems: Ventilation, Mechanic Exhaustion and Refrigeration.

The installed systems and equipments include from a simple exchanger of heat, conventional installations for comfort of the type "self-contained" or frozen water for residences, commercial buildings, hotels, stores, banks, shopping centers, supermarkets, to specific installations of industrial processes, laboratories, clean rooms, hospitals, telecommunication stations.

The success gotten from the done work has brought more comfort to our customers, mainly in installations with bigger complexity.

PRO DAC AR CONDICIONADO LTDA, has "know-how" in installations of TERMOACUMULAÇÃO equipments, units coolers with centrifugal compressors, water/air "chillers" of condensation and water/air conditioning units type "self-contained" and units type "split-system".

It also has "know-how" in intelligent systems of temperature control, humidity and air quality, supervision and management along with the lighting system, fire, security, access control, elevator, etc, being all the controlled and monitored management through graphical screens operated by microcomputers.
